Description

III is a 2D platformer game. The protagonist is Hannie, the princess of Meeria, who has powers that can color anything. Her adventure starts with an evil witch suddenly appearing and stealing something precious from Hannie. This results is her losing control of her own powers, and Meeria is starting to lose its colors. Hannie must defeat the witch and recolor Meeria.
